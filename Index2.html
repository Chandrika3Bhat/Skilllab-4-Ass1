BUDDHA

The image of Buddha often represents peace, serenity, and tranquility. Having a Buddha frame in your home can serve as a visual reminder to cultivate inner peace and calmness in your daily life.

LORD GANESHA

In Hindu tradition, Ganesha is considered a guardian deity who protects homes and families from harm and negative energies. Keeping a Ganesha frame in the house is believed to create a protective aura and foster a sense of security and harmony.

HORSE

Horses are often associated with qualities such as strength, power, and vitality. Having a horse frame in your house can serve as a visual reminder of these attributes, inspiring you to tackle challenges with vigor and determination.
